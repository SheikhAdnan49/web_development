Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

This course delves into the fascinating field of positive psychology, focusing on the science behind happiness and well-being. Students will explore the psychological principles, theories, and research findings related to happiness, resilience, and life satisfaction. Through interactive lectures, discussions, and experiential exercises, participants will gain insights into the factors that contribute to personal happiness and learn practical strategies for enhancing well-being in their own lives.

“ Understand the concept of happiness from a psychological perspective. Explore the theories and models of well-being and life satisfaction. Examine the factors that influence happiness, including genetics, personality, and life circumstances. Learn evidence-based strategies for increasing happiness and resilience. ”

Tomas Alexander

This course content provides a comprehensive exploration of the psychological principles underlying happiness and well-being, equipping students with practical tools and strategies for leading more fulfilling lives.



Examine the ethical and societal implications of AI technologies. Analyze case studies and real-world applications of AI across various industries. Develop critical thinking skills to evaluate the strengths, limitations, and future directions of AI.
